Spaghetti

A fully prepared spaghetti dish.

Spaghetti is an italian inspired dish that can be served in a variety of ways with an infinite combination of toppings! Often served with garlic bread.

Recipe

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Red sauce
  • Toppings (Optional)
  • Garlic Bread (Optional)

Steps

  1. Bring 4 quarts of water to a boil.
  2. Add spaghetti to the water and mix every so often for 7 minutes.
  3. Drain water from spaghetti.
  4. Add desired amount of red sauce.
  5. Serve on a plate with any extra desired toppings + garlic bread.
