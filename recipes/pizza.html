Pizza

A delicous baked pizza pie with pepperoni toppings!

Pizza is an italian inspired dish consisting of a dough base with red sauce on top followed by cheese and an assortment of toppings.

Recipe

  • Pizza dough
  • Red sauce
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Toppings (ex. pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms)

Steps

  1. Spead the pizza dough to the desired thickness and size.
  2. Apply a layer of red sauce on top of the dough.
  3. Apply a layer of shredded cheese on top of the sauce.
  4. Add the rest of your desired toppings to the pizza.
  5. Bake in over at 400 degrees for 10 minutes.
  6. Cut pizza into slices and enjoy!
