Waffles

Waffles covered in butter and syrup!

Waffles are a simple and easy breakfast dish that has an infinite number of ways you can enjoy it!

Recipe

  • Pancake or Waffle Mix
  • Milk
  • Cooking oil
  • Egg
  • Butter (Optional)
  • Syrup (Optional)

Steps

  1. Add 1 cup of mix.
  2. Add 3/4 cup of milk.
  3. Add 1 tsp of cooking oil.
  4. Add 1 egg.
  5. Stir the mix thoroughly
  6. Place a half cup of mix into the waffle maker and flip.
  7. Wait for waffle maker to indicate it is done and remove the waffle.
  8. Add desired toppings, normally apply butter to the waffles and drizzle syrup on top.
